# course/

Objective 1 — the JD-validated FDE course. The full curriculum is still a Stage-4 initiative
(gated on Stage-2 traction, Gate B of the
[roadmap](../docs/plans/2026-06-20-001-feat-fde-os-staged-roadmap-plan.md)), but the directory is
no longer empty scaffolding — the *validation inputs* are live and growing:

- **`target-jds/`** — the ground truth. Real job descriptions (Google · Reflection · CVS) that the
  course is validated against, compiled into competency profiles by
  [`jd-compiler`](../skills/jd-compiler/).
- **`prep/`** — per-JD prep curricula and readiness scorecards, plus a real interview loop preserved
  as a case study ([Google FDE loop, 2026-07-07](prep/google-fde-loop-prep-2026-07-07.md)) and the
  [`coding-drill-kit`](prep/tools/coding-drill-kit/) (eval-as-gate pointed at a human).
- **`market/`** — competitive intel on how others teach FDE work (e.g. the
  [Maven FDE bootcamp](market/2026-07-02-maven-fde-bootcamp.md), reverse-engineered; its
  production stack was adopted into the jd-compiler taxonomy).

The course thesis stays the same one question: *given a JD, does this material train a person well
enough to land that job?*
