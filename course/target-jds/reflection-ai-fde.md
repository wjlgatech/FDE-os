# Target JD — Reflection AI · Forward Deployed Engineer

A **course-validation target** (Objective 1): the prep curriculum
([`../prep/reflection-ai-fde-prep.md`](../prep/reflection-ai-fde-prep.md)) is judged by one
question — *would someone who completes it be ready to land this role?*

**Source:** https://jobs.ashbyhq.com/reflectionai (Ashby board; fetched 2026-06-23).
**Company mission:** *"build open superintelligence and make it accessible to all."*
**Location:** New York (SF option). **Comp:** not disclosed.

Reflection posts **two** FDE variants; this target captures both. The primary prep track is the
**AI Engineer** role; the **Post-training Lead** is a stretch/senior track.

## Variant A — Forward Deployed Engineer, AI Engineer (primary target)

The canonical FDE: embed with enterprise customers, architect and ship agentic systems in their
environment.

**Responsibilities (verbatim signal):**
- *"Partner with strategists and sales to understand enterprise needs and architect agentic solutions"*
- *"Build agentic systems using advanced models, orchestrating LLM workflows and integrating with enterprise infrastructure"*
- *"Collaborate with research teams to adapt and fine-tune models for customer requirements"*
- *"Support deployments across hybrid environments (public cloud, VPC, on-premises)"*
- *"Contribute to evolving playbooks and best practices"*

**Required:**
- Strong SWE background shipping **production systems (Python, TypeScript)**
- Deploying enterprise software in **cloud/hybrid environments with DevOps practices**
- Deep ML with hands-on **modern AI stack: vector databases, RAG, agents, evaluations, fine-tuning**
- **3+ years** delivering AI-driven enterprise solutions
- **Customer-facing** ability; **high agency** in fast-paced startups

## Variant B — Forward Deployed Engineer, Lead · LLM Post-training (stretch)

Leads post-training engagements end-to-end inside customer environments.

**Responsibilities:** lead post-training engagements; design RL training environments (synthetic
data, reward modeling); build evaluation infrastructure + success metrics; own data pipelines
(raw customer data → training-ready); deploy post-trained models across hybrid environments;
mentor + scale the practice.

**Required:** post-training at scale (**50B+ param models**); built/operated **RL environments,
preference optimization, reward models, verifiers**; deep **eval methodology**; **multi-node GPU /
distributed training**; **6+ years** (incl. 2+ yrs LLM post-training in leadership); customer-facing
experience or genuine interest.

## The FDE signal (why this is a true FDE role, not a consultant)

Both variants are *one customer, many capabilities* (Palantir's Delta shape): embedded, deploying
*inside* the customer's perimeter (VPC/on-prem), architecting under ambiguity from a sales/strategy
handoff, feeding learnings back into "evolving playbooks." The customer-facing discovery half is
exactly what the [Delta Discovery Protocol](../../field-kits/delta-discovery-protocol/SKILL.md)
teaches — so FDE-os's own content is part of the prep.

## Competency clusters (the prep curriculum maps to these)

1. Production SWE — Python **and** TypeScript
2. Enterprise / hybrid deployment + DevOps (cloud, VPC, on-prem)
3. Modern AI stack — RAG, vector DBs, evaluations, fine-tuning
4. Agentic system design — LLM-workflow orchestration, enterprise integration
5. The FDE craft — discovery, customer-facing architecting, high agency
6. (stretch) Post-training — RL envs, reward modeling, evals at scale, distributed training
