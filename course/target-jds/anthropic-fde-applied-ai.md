## Role
Member of the **Applied AI** team who **embeds directly with strategic customers** to drive
transformational AI adoption. White-glove deployment of Anthropic products in enterprise
environments; **identify and codify repeatable deployment patterns** and feed insights back to
Product & Engineering.

## Responsibilities
- Work **inside customer systems** to build **production applications with Claude**, meeting customer requirements.
- Deliver technical artifacts customers run in production: **MCP servers, sub-agents, and agent skills**.
- Codify repeatable deployment patterns; contribute field insight back to Product/Engineering.
- Represent Anthropic at the highest level in customer environments.

## Requirements
- **3+ years** in a technical, **customer-facing** role (FDE, or SWE with consulting experience).
- **Production LLM experience**: advanced prompt engineering, **agent development**, **evaluation frameworks**, deployment at scale.
- Strong **Python** (ideally + TypeScript/Java); has **shipped production applications**.

## Attributes (soft signals they screen for)
- Operate **autonomously**; **thrive under ambiguity**.
- **High-cooperation** cross-org collaboration; balance competing priorities with integrity.
- Passion for **safe, beneficial AI** through creative technical application.
