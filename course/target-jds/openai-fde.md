## Role
Lead **complex end-to-end deployments of frontier models in production** alongside strategic
customers. Turn research breakthroughs into production systems.

## Responsibilities
- **Own discovery → technical scoping → system design → build → production rollout**, with customer engineering and domain teams.
- Measure success by **production adoption, measurable workflow impact, and eval-driven feedback** that changes product/model roadmaps.
- Forge/manage relationships with **customer leadership and stakeholders**.
- **Scope work, sequence delivery, remove blockers early** — trade off scope / speed / quality.
- **Contribute directly in the code** when clarity or momentum depends on it.
- Share **field feedback** with Research and Product to influence the model/product.

## Requirements
- **5+ years** engineering or technical deployment, ideally customer-facing or government.
- Scoped and delivered **complex systems in fast-moving / ambiguous** contexts.
- **Built/deployed LLM or generative systems**; understand how **model behavior affects product experience**.
- **Simplify complexity; fast, sound decisions under pressure**; communicate clearly across engineers, product, customer stakeholders.
- Write/review **production-grade code, frontend and backend** — **Python, JavaScript**, or similar.

## Work model
Hybrid, **3 days/week in office**; relocation assistance. Works with Product, Research, Partnerships, GRC, Security, GTM.

## Competency-cluster signal (FDE-os taxonomy)
`fde_craft` (**own discovery→rollout**, ambiguity, remove blockers) · `production_swe` (prod-grade
**frontend + backend**, Python/JS) · `enterprise_deploy` (production adoption, gov) · `modern_ai_stack`
(**eval-driven**, model-behavior→product) · `agentic_design` (LLM/gen systems in prod) · `leadership`
(customer-leadership relationships, trade-offs, field-feedback to Research/Product). **Distinctive:**
more senior (**5+**), explicit **scope/speed/quality trade-off** ownership, **frontend + backend**,
**eval-driven roadmap influence**.
