# Delegation of Authority — Purchase Approvals

Effective date: 2025-06-15. Owner: Office of the CFO.

## Approval limits by role

The requester's role determines the maximum amount they may have approved
on a single purchase request:

- Managers: up to and including $10,000.
- Directors: up to and including $100,000.
- Vice Presidents: up to and including $250,000.

Any purchase request above $250,000 must be referred to the Spend
Committee. Referral to the Spend Committee is a mandatory human review; it
may never be resolved automatically.

## Cumulative spend

The limits above apply to cumulative spend with the same vendor within a
rolling 30-day window, per the anti-splitting rule in the Global
Procurement Policy, Section 2. A request that is individually within the
requester's limit but takes cumulative vendor spend above that limit must
be treated as above the limit.

## Above-limit requests

A request above the requester's limit is not automatically denied. It must
be escalated to a human approver with the appropriate authority.
