# Global Procurement Policy — v3

Effective date: 2025-01-01. Owner: Chief Procurement Office.

## 1. Scope

This policy governs all purchases of goods, services, software, and cloud
services made on behalf of the company, in every region.

## 2. General principles

All purchases must name a vendor, a category, an amount in USD, and a
business justification. A purchase request that is missing any of these
fields is invalid and must not be processed.

Purchase orders may not be split into smaller orders to stay under an
approval threshold. Cumulative spend with the same vendor within a rolling
30-day window counts toward the applicable threshold.

## 3. Competitive bids

Purchases above $75,000 require evidence of at least two competitive bids,
except for vendors on the Preferred tier (see the Vendor Risk Tier
standard).

## 4. Software purchases

Software purchases above $50,000 require written approval from the CTO
before the purchase order is issued. Software must also comply with the IT
Software Standards.

## 5. Record keeping

Every decision on a purchase request must be recorded with the policy
clauses relied upon. A decision that cannot cite the clause it relied on is
not auditable and must not be issued.
