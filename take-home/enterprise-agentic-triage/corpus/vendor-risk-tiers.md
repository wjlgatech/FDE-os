# Vendor Risk Tier Standard

Effective date: 2025-09-01. Owner: Third-Party Risk Management.

Every vendor is assigned a risk tier in the vendor master database. The
tier determines the diligence required before any purchase:

## Preferred

Pre-approved vendors with current diligence on file. Purchases proceed
under the normal approval limits. Preferred vendors are exempt from the
competitive-bid requirement.

## Standard

Vendors in good standing. Purchases proceed under the normal approval
limits with standard diligence.

## High-Risk

Vendors with open findings, adverse media, or operations in sanctioned
regions. Any purchase from a High-Risk vendor requires enhanced due
diligence and must be escalated to Third-Party Risk Management for human
review before approval, regardless of amount.

## Prohibited

Vendors that have failed diligence or are subject to sanctions. Purchases
from Prohibited vendors must be denied. No role may approve a purchase
from a Prohibited vendor, and no escalation path exists.

## Unknown vendors

A vendor that does not appear in the vendor master database has no tier.
Purchases from unknown vendors must be escalated for vendor onboarding;
they may not be approved automatically.
